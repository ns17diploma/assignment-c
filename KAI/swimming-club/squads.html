RSC Swimming Club

RSC Swimming Club has a number of Squads.

These are defined as detailed below:

  1. Tadpoles - This is a beginner group for the under 10 year olds learning to swim
  2. Frogs - This is for the more mature learner wanting to take up swimming
  3. Minnows - This is the first of the skill development for those who can swim
  4. Piranahs - This is for the more serious swimmer competing at District level
  5. Sharks - For the serious competitor
  6. Synchro - This is our Synchronised Swimming Squad led by one of the top coaches in the country
  7. Masters - This squad is for swimmers aged over 25
